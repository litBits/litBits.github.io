Hardware Guide

Materials

  • x1 Arduino Uno
  • x1 Full-Size Breadboard
  • Jumper Cables
    • x2 Short
    • x8 Long
  • Wires
    • x7 Red
    • x8 Black
  • x8 5mm, 2-Pin LEDs
  • x8 PNP Transistors (PN 2222)

The First Cell

  1. Power Line--Red wire from 1d to 37e (H right-shift one from power line, V down-shift 9) (power line to power line)
  2. Transistor (looking at flat face)
    1. Left (in) pin to 1b (H left-shift two from power line) (power line)
    2. Mid (switch) pin to 3b (V down-shift 2) (switch line)
    3. Right (out) pin to 5b (V down-shift 2) (junction line)
  3. LED
    1. Long (in) pin to 5a (H left-shift one from transistor out pin) (junction line)
    2. b. Short (out) pin to 6a (V down-shift 2) (ground line)
  4. Ground Line--Black wire from 6e to ‘-’ strip (ground line to ground strip)

The Mid Cells

  1. For the 2nd - 7th LED cell, repeat steps 1-4.

The Final Cell

  1. For the 8th and final LED cell, repeat steps 2-4.
